# Outreach Draft Rubric (tvt-sales-engine job-4 drafts)

Gate rubric for the single most outward-facing text in the system: outreach drafts.
These are ALWAYS drafts — the human sends (the router has no send path by design).
This gate runs before the draft is even staged for review, so the human reviews
quality, not slop.

## Criterion 1: POV-anchored, never "checking in" (weight: 5)

**What to look for:** The draft leads with the account-specific POV or a researched
trigger (their announcement, filing, market event) — the Challenger "warmer". Zero
"just checking in" / "circling back" / "hope you're well" filler openings.

**Score 1.0:** Opens with something only THIS recipient could receive.
**Score 0.5:** Personalized surface over a generic ask.
**Score 0.0:** Template blast any recipient could get.

## Criterion 2: No unverified claims (weight: 5)

**What to look for:** Every fact about the recipient's company traces to
fact-checked research. Names, roles, and company facts verified — hallucinated
personalization to a real executive is the highest-blast-radius failure this
system can produce.

**Score 1.0:** All recipient-facts trace to gated research; names/roles verified.
**Score 0.5:** Plausible but unverified detail present.
**Score 0.0:** Any fabricated-looking specific (wrong role, invented initiative,
misattributed quote).

## Criterion 3: No pricing, no overcommitment (weight: 4)

**What to look for:** No pricing anywhere. No delivery/scope commitments a human
hasn't made. CTA is a conversation (session, workshop, 20 minutes), never a
contract-shaped ask.

**Score 1.0:** Clean — conversation-shaped ask only.
**Score 0.5:** Soft implied commitments ("we'll deliver X in Y weeks").
**Score 0.0:** Pricing or explicit commitments present.

## Criterion 4: Brevity and per-persona fit (weight: 3)

**What to look for:** Short enough to be read (target under ~150 words for cold
touch); tuned to the persona (C-level gets business outcome framing, practitioner
gets specifics); objection-aware without being defensive.

**Score 1.0:** Tight, persona-matched, one clear ask.
**Score 0.5:** Right content, 2x too long or persona-flat.
**Score 0.0:** A memo, not an outreach note.

## Criterion 5: Draft posture (weight: 3)

**What to look for:** Output is explicitly staged as a draft for human review —
carries what to verify before sending (names, facts, timing) rather than
pretending to be send-ready.

**Score 1.0:** Draft status + verify-before-send list present.
**Score 0.5:** Draft framing present but nothing flagged for verification.
**Score 0.0:** Presented as ready-to-send.

## Gate threshold

Weighted score ≥ 0.85 to pass. On FAIL: remediate once, re-gate; still failing →
surface to the user with the eval record. Never ship silently.
