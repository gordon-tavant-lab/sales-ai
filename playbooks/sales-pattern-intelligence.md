## The Meta-Pattern: How Enterprise Deals Actually Work

> **Enterprise services sales is not about selling. It's about creating the conditions for a
> "yes" to become inevitable.**

The formula:

```
Relationship depth × demonstrated capability × client urgency × low perceived risk = deal
```

Each variable has levers. The best salespeople manipulate all four simultaneously.
