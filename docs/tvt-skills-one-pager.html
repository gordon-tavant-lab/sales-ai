Sales Workflow Recipes — Chaining tvt-* Skills

spec 007 · sales-skills marketplace plugin · how to stack/pipe skills for a sales goal
HUNT — net-new logo, prospect → pitch
Goal: build a qualified pipeline and get to a first pitch
tvt-intel-dispatch→tvt-intel-deep→tvt-intel-factcheck→tvt-sales-pitch→tvt-create-deck→tvt-sales-pack
Fan out across the whole target list, deep-dive whoever surfaces as a real fit, verify every claim before it's client-facing, then turn the story into a branded, presales-ready pack.
EXPAND — quarterly business review
Goal: walk into a QBR with a signal-backed narrative
tvt-intel-qbr→tvt-core-lookup→tvt-os-contrarian→tvt-create-deck
Triangulate internal + industry + peer signal, cross-reference it against the existing portfolio/playbook library, red-team the story before the client sees it, then build the deck.
DEFENSIBLE POV — any client meeting
Goal: never bring an unverified point of view into a client room
tvt-intel-deep→tvt-intel-factcheck→tvt-os-judge→tvt-create-explainer
Factcheck is the mandatory gate before any POV leaves the building; judge catches drift in the artifact itself; the explainer format describes first and argues last.
COMPETITIVE DISPLACEMENT
Goal: win a deal where a named competitor is already in the room
tvt-intel-fanout→tvt-core-extract→tvt-os-contrarian→tvt-sales-pitch
Research every competitor/vendor named in the transcripts, pull the pain-point mappings out of that research, stress-test the displacement argument, then write the pitch.
FLYWHEEL — turn a win into reusable IP
Goal: one client's pattern becomes everyone's accelerator
tvt-intel-flywheel→tvt-core-write→tvt-sales-distill
Extract the pattern that repeated across 2+ clients, write it up as a canonical playbook doc, then fold it into the weekly sales-pattern sharpening pass.

Overlay layer — applies on top of any chain above, not a step you add yourself

tvt-os-metacognition — wraps any step and auto-routes to contrarian and/or judge, picking light vs. deep by stakes, instead of hand-picking one.
tvt-gov-guard — redacts/masks CRM PII the instant it enters any chain — fires automatically, never a manual step.
tvt-gov-attest — hash-chained decision ledger behind tvt-core-mcts/tvt-os-judge outputs, when a decision needs to be backtested later.
Recipes are illustrative starting points, not the only valid order — swap/drop steps per deal. Full skill detail: page 1 & CAPABILITIES.md. 2026-07-06 · page 2 of 2
