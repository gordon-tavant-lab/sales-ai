# Tavant Template Layout Guide

Full descriptions of each layout in the Tavant template, with guidance on when to use each.

## Title Slides & Dividers

### `Title Slide_B` (Slide 0)
- **Use for:** Opening cover slide only
- **Placeholders:** Title (ALL CAPS), Date/Event text, optional subtitle
- **Notes:** This is the official Tavant title slide. Use for the first slide of every deck.

### `BREAKER SLIDE 2` / `BREAKER SLIDE 3` / `BREAKER SLIDE 4` (Slides 2–4)
- **Use for:** Section dividers between major topics
- **Placeholders:** Title (ALL CAPS breaker heading)
- **Notes:** Three visual variants — alternate them for visual variety across a long deck. Use one at the start of each major section.

### `Thank You` (Slide 22)
- **Use for:** Final closing slide (always)
- **Pre-populated with:** "THANK YOU", tavant.com, hello@tavant.com
- **Notes:** Keep as-is — just verify the contact info is correct. This slide is already complete in the template.

## Agenda

### `Agenda` (Slide 1)
- **Use for:** Table of contents / meeting agenda (2nd slide, after Title Slide)
- **Placeholders:** Title ("AGENDA"), bullet list of agenda items
- **Notes:** The template shows 5 agenda items — add/remove bullets to match actual count.

---

## Content Slides

### `Title + Content` (Slide 5)
- **Use for:** Standard content slide — title + body text/bullets
- **Placeholders:** Title, Content (body area)
- **Notes:** Most versatile layout. Good for: feature explanations, process steps, bulleted lists, key points.

### `Title + Subtitle + Content` (Slide 6)
- **Use for:** Content slide where a subtitle clarifies the context
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle (italic/muted), Content (body)
- **Notes:** Use subtitle for things like "Phase 1" or "Current State" that frame the main content.

### `Title + 2-Column Content` (Slide 7)
- **Use for:** Side-by-side comparison, two independent topic areas
- **Placeholders:** Title, optional Subtitle, Content Left, Content Right
- **Notes:** Great for Before/After, Problem/Solution, or two parallel workstreams.

### `Title + Subtitle` (Slide 9)
- **Use for:** Transition slide with just a title and one sentence
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle
- **Notes:** Use as a "statement slide" — a single bold assertion or key takeaway.

### `Title Only` (Slide 8) and `Title Only - Grey` (Slide 11)
- **Use for:** Full canvas slide where you're building custom content
- **Placeholders:** Title only
- **Notes:** Use when the content needs a custom layout (large stats, icon grid, etc.) and you'll build shapes manually. Grey variant for secondary/supporting sections.

### `Title + Content - Grey` (Slide 12)
- **Use for:** Supporting/secondary content slide in muted grey styling
- **Placeholders:** Title, Content
- **Notes:** Use to visually differentiate supporting data from primary content.

## Image Slides

### `Image + Content A` (Slide 17)
- **Use for:** Large visual (left column) + narrative/bullets (right column)
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, Picture (large, left side), Content (right side)
- **Notes:** Best for showing a product screenshot, diagram, or photo alongside description.

### `Image + Content B` (Slide 14)
- **Use for:** Small image (left) + two numbered callout items (right)
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, Picture, two text callout areas
- **Notes:** Good for case study format: small logo/image on left, key outcomes on right.

### `Image + Content C` (Slide 15)
- **Use for:** Full-width top image + 3-column icon/text grid below
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, Picture (top), 3 icon+text pairs
- **Notes:** Good for platform/product overview slides.

### `Images + Content D` (Slide 18)
- **Use for:** Three images across the top, each with a caption/description
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, 3 Pictures, 3 Text areas
- **Notes:** Great for team profiles, three parallel case studies, or a before/during/after sequence.

## Special Layouts

### `Multi-Case Study` (Slide 13)
- **Use for:** Multiple client logos + brief outcome descriptions
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, 6 Picture placeholders (client logos), 4 Text areas
- **Notes:** Classic "our clients" or "proven results" layout. Fill all image spots with logos.

### `Multi-Quote` (Slide 16)
- **Use for:** Three testimonial quotes with speaker photos
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, 3 Pictures (headshots), 3 Quote text areas
- **Notes:** Use for client testimonials or third-party validation.

### `Content + Chart` (Slide 19)
- **Use for:** Data visualization + narrative insight
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, Chart, Key Takeaway text, Chart description
- **Notes:** Chart is embedded in the template. Edit chart data via the PPTX chart data table. Use when you have quantitative data to show.

### `Timeline 1` (Slide 20)
- **Use for:** Growth/progress timeline with milestone markers and values
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, Year labels, milestone text, value callouts
- **Notes:** Best for showing investment growth, adoption curves, or milestone achievements. Pre-formatted with years on x-axis.

### `Timeline 2` (Slide 21)
- **Use for:** Linear project/process timeline with phase descriptions
- **Placeholders:** Title, Subtitle, 7 Date labels, 7 Description text areas
- **Notes:** Best for roadmaps, project phases, or implementation sequences.
